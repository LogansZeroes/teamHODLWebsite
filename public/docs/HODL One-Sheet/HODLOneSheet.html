We are proud to present the world’s first Crypto Rap album HODL, a 6-track ode to the cryptocurrency rabbit hole and the adventures navigating it. Hashbrown, a San Francisco based rapper, leads us on our trip through the cryptosphere over certified slaps produced by  The Cluff, out of San Jose. Memes, acronyms, and economic concepts all get brought to life in this conceptual album; one of these is where we drew our name from.  HODL = Hold On for Dear Life, which can apply to crypto investment but also life in general, having faith when the chips are down that the tables will come back around. It is from this ethos that we have crafted this project.

True to crypto fashion, we examined current methods of fundraising and found what we believe to be a better way. This has led us to develop a concept for a HODL album ICO which will launch October 18th. It will combine all the best aspects of crowdfunding and crypto investing.
