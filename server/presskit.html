Memes, acronyms, and economic concepts all get brought to life in this conceptual album; one of these is where we drew our name from.

HODL = Hold On for Dear Life, which can apply to crypto investment but also life in general, having faith when the chips are down that the tables will come back around. It is from this ethos that we have crafted this project.

This has led us to develop a concept for a HODL album ICO which will launch October 18th. It will combine all the best aspects of crowdfunding and crypto investing. For more info read our white paper!
